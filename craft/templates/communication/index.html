What we can build for you

Icons

Create a shared visual language for your team and your stakeholders. Use simple icons to represent people, places, concepts and things from your project. Reuse them in documents, emails, slides, intranets, desktop wallpapers, websites, on posters or on mouse pads.

Visual slide packs

Your audience can only do one thing at a time: listen or read. Wordy slides are hurting your message (and people's brains). Transform your slides into simple, clear, visuals that compliment what you've got to say and really show people what you mean.

Animated videos

Tell the story of your case for change, your new vision, strategy, product, or business process, in a fun and memorable way. Cut through the clutter of verbal information and produce a short animation to share your key messages.

Infographics

Clearly communicate complex information, what a 'day in the life' of a new role looks like or how a change will be rolled out. Emails and briefings are easily lost, forgotten, or both. Make information visual with infographics that make important information easier to digest.
